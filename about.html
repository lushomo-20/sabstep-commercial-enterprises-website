Company Background

Sabstep Commercial Enterprises Limited was established in May 1992 and incorporated in Zambia on 8 September 1998. Over the years, the company has grown into one of Zambia?s largest service providers to government ministries, mining firms, public and private companies, and societies.

We are known for steady growth, meaningful contributions to the Zambian economy, and the creation of jobs and careers. Our specialization spans commercial printing, construction, mechanical engineering, and the supply and service of industrial equipment and accessories.
